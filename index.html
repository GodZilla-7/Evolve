We're EVOLVE

Evolve, originating from MANIT, stands as a beacon in electric vehicle innovation. With a focus on sustainability, it garners awards nationally. Through collaborative efforts, students engineer cutting-edge designs, earning acclaim. Evolve isn't just about accolades; it's a community, fostering passion for clean energy. Together, members push boundaries, inspiring others to join the movement towards a greener tomorrow.
